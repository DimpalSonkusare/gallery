Badminton rules were formulated and the sport was standardized in Europe, but most of the prominent players belong to China, Malaysia, and Indonesia. However, the sport is loved and widely played in Europe too. Players win points by participating in graded tournaments. The World Championships and the Olympics determine the world champion of the year.
